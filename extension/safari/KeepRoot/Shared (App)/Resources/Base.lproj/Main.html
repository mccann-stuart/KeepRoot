Safari Extension

KeepRoot for Safari

Enable the extension, then connect it to your KeepRoot backend from the extension settings inside Safari.

On iPhone and iPad, turn on KeepRoot in Settings, then Apps, Safari, Extensions.

  1. Open Settings, then Apps, Safari, Extensions.
  2. Turn on KeepRoot and choose which websites it can access.
  3. Open Safari and use the extension menu to save pages into KeepRoot.

Safari may ask for per-site permission the first time you use the extension.

The extension is installed. Open Safari Settings to turn it on.

The extension is on. Open Safari and use the toolbar button to save pages into KeepRoot.

The extension is off. Turn it on in Safari Settings before saving pages.

  1. Open Safari Settings.
  2. Enable KeepRoot in the Extensions tab.
  3. Open the extension settings and add your Worker URL and API key.
